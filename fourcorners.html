Abstract

Intended for Cynthia Barnett's Adventure Journalism course, "36.998976, -109.045172," or simply "Four Corners," certainly demonstrates the spatial theories discussed throughout the project. This piece, however, focuses more on the natural and cultural history of place, taking Barnett's Rain as a model.
