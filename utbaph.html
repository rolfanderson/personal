Abstract

It's hard to add anything to the significance of Pizza Hut that wasn't already covered by the brilliant episode of 99% Invisible, but I still include this section for fun. Used to Be a Pizza Huts, or UTBAPHs, enter the conversation as reminders of the humor possible in theory; even the name UTBAPH comes from a stand-up comedy routine. Challenge: think of the most inappropriate repurposing of a defunct Pizza Hut. I stick with crematorium. Add to the humor the potent metaphor these former Pizza Huts deliver. The rewriting of space like a hard drive. The recycling of its exterior shell like a hermit crab. [...]
